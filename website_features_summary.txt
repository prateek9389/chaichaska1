========================================================================
                      CHAICO OPERATIONAL OVERVIEW & FEATURES
========================================================================

ChaiCo is a state-of-the-art corporate chai delivery and subscription web
application, built using Next.js, React, and custom Tailwind/Vanilla CSS.
It features a premium visual aesthetic designed around a custom White and
Dark Chocolate brand color palette.

Below is the complete feature map, page directory, and portal breakdown
for the platform.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. USER-FACING FRONTEND ROUTES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Home Page (/)
  - Elegant landing experience welcoming corporate members.
  - Quick navigation links to shop menu, wallet, and subscriptions.

* Shop Menu (/shop)
  - Interactive grid displaying hot brews (Masala Chai, Ginger Chai, etc.).
  - Add-ons Selector checklist (Organic Saffron, Extra Ginger, Honey).
  - Combo Creator allowing users to select standard pairings.

* Corporate Subscriptions (/subscribe)
  - Subscription tier cards (Daily Kadak, Twice a Day, Chai+Snacks, Team Plan).
  - Desk/floor delivery scheduling options.

* Wallet Ledger (/wallet)
  - Prepaid corporate wallet panel showing current balances.
  - Credit/Debit recharge mock interfaces.

* Secure Checkout (/checkout)
  - Office building and room selection matrix.
  - Delivery time scheduler, order totals, and checkout dispatch buttons.

* Order Queue Trackers (/orders & /orders/[id])
  - Real-time order progress timeline (Brewing, Dispatched, Served).
  - Wait-time delivery estimations configured by the kitchen.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. BREWMASTER OUTLET PORTAL (/chaimaker)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
A comprehensive workspace for kitchen operators to manage active shifts,
inventory, and dispatch logs.

* Order Queue Tab:
  - Three-column priority kanban board (High, Medium, Low priority cards).
  - Live wait-time countdown timers.
  - Priority toggles and preparation time allocator inputs on each card.

* Today's Prep Tab:
  - Aggregated cup numbers grouped by tea type (e.g. 45 Cups Ginger Chai, 30 Cups Saffron Chai) with add-on checklist summaries.

* Stocks & Inventory:
  - Live ingredient stock quantities editor.
  - RESTOCK ALERT broadcaster form sending material requests to Admin.

* Subscriptions Dispatch:
  - Corporate subscription ledger displaying floor/desk locations.
  - Daily dispatch scheduler checksheets.

* Feedback Hub:
  - Customer review tables alongside rating metrics.

* Leave Request Roster:
  - Operator time-off forms detailing reason, dates, and approval status.

* Brewmaster Profile:
  - Identity managers (Name, contact, outlet details) and secure logouts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. ADMINISTRATIVE OVERLORDS PORTAL (/admin)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
A command center managing multi-building logistics, vendor metrics, and
financial ledgers.

* Secure Gateway Entry:
  - Dual-credential security barrier requiring both PIN (9999) and Password (admin123).

* Left Sidebar Navigation Tabs (10 Sections):
  1. Overview: Wealthio financial bento overview of the entire business.
  2. Buildings: Desk coverage, user count, and revenue of office towers.
  3. Vendors: preparation times and rating matrices.
  4. Subscriptions: Pricing and plans editor.
  5. Offers: Active coupon campaigns and coupon usages tracker.
  6. Disputes: Support ticket log and resolution triggers.
  7. Users & Wallets: Corporate profile logs with block/unblock toggles.
  8. Restock Alerts: Inbound material requests from brewmasters with dispatch actions.
  9. Leaves Roster: staff leave requests scheduler with Approve/Reject toggles.
  10. Global Settings: System operational window settings and system-wide broadcaster.

* Right Sidebar Quick Feeds:
  - Today's Live Revenue Progress Bar: Tracking current sales against daily goals.
  - Active Orders List: Real-time look at cooking/delivery states.
  - System Announcements Feed: Logs alert updates, leaves, and stock alerts.

* 20-Section Overview Dashboard Bento Box:
  1. Tea Sales Revenue: Total revenue tracker.
  2. Material/Ingredient Procurements: Expenses incurred for ingredients.
  3. Net Operational Profits: Total net profits.
  4. Sales Volume Overview: Interactive bar graph tracking monthly cups sold.
  5. Chai Ingredient Expense Split: Segmented chart showing milk, leaves, spices costs.
  6. Station/Vendor Procurement Card: Virtual transaction card details for Jaipur HQ.
  7. Recent Orders Table: Searchable and sortable invoice transactions tracker.
  8. Cost Per Cup Breakdown: itemized costs per cup (Milk, spices, packaging).
  9. Building-wise Procurement Card: Expenses broken down per office block.
  10. Monthly Profit & Loss Summary: Snapshot of sales, expenses, and margins.
  11. Ingredient Stock Value: Inventory depot reserves values.
  12. Vendor Payout Card: Paid vs outstanding vendor balances tracker.
  13. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): User marketing metrics.
  14. Average Order Value (AOV): Mean ticket size tracking.
  15. Refunds & Cancellations Summary: Order cancellations tracking rate.
  16. Building Occupancy vs Order Ratio: Order density metrics.
  17. Subscription Renewal Rate: Renewal gauge progress indicator.
  18. Wallet Recharge Trend: Daily recharge figures.
  19. Top Expense Categories: Rents, utility, and labor overheads.
  20. Delivery/Prep Time Efficiency Card: Average speed efficiency ratings.
========================================================================
